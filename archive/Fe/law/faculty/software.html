Software

Any program need by law school faculty, staff, of students can be downloaded from our own software page. But you can also get if from Northwestern's Software and Documentation site for full download support..

NU Law faculty should probably have the following programs loaded on their computer. The IS department supports them and they could come in handy.

  • MS Office 97
  • Meeting Maker
  • McAfee Virus Scan
  • Lexis Nexis
  • Firstclass
  • Netscape
  • WestLaw
  • Eudora
  • Write Fax

For a funky alternative you can also check out Northwestern University's Virtual Store.
