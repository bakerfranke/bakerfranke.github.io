Personal Web Pages:

Pubweb.nwu.edu provides Northwestern students, faculty, and staff a platform for personal experimentation with the WWW,
on a modest scale, by serving up personal information. The Pubweb FAQ site should be able to answer any questions you have about publishing your own personal web pages.
