Amendment to the Constitution for the creation of a High School Music Committee

 

Purpose: To collect and organize music and/or other forms of entertainment for playing in between classes over the high school intercom system.

 

I. Membership

 

A. Committee Head(s)

1.) The head(s) will be elected by the entire student body.

2. Each head will have equal power

3. The head(s) will share responsibilities equally

 

B. The Faculty Advisor

  • 1. The faculty advisor will assist as well as advise in the workings of the committee.

    2. In the event of unresolved differences between the committee heads, the faculty advisor will be the deciding vote.

    •

    • C. There will be no grade representatives to this committee

     

     

    II. Responsibilities of the Music Committee Heads

  • A. The head(s) shall select Music or other forms of auditory entertainment for playing over the intercom system each day.

    B. The head(s) shall prepare the music for playing each morning before the beginning of classes.

    C. Said music or entertainment will be of an appropriate nature for students between 6th and 12th grade.

    D. Heads should be open to student suggestion for entertainment and create an itinerary to satisfy those requests.
