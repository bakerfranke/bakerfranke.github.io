Oh goody! A survey! JOY

That’s right! IS will be conducting faculty and staff training. But to make it as painless as possible IS wants you to rate yourself on various applications to figure out exactly what kind of training you want and need.

Here’s how it works:
For each application listed there are a series of subjects pertaining to that application with the numbers one (1) through five (5) listed. If you feel that you are good at a certain subject you should place your dot in the number one (1) space. If, however, you feel that you are bad at a certain subject you should place your dot in the number five (5) space. Of course you don’t have to say whether you’re really bad, or really good, that’s what two (2), three (3) and four (4) are there for.

So it really works like this:

  • If you’re really good at something and/or you don’t want training in that subject put your dot in, or towards, the number one (1) spot.
  • If you’re really bad at a certain subject and/or you really want training place a dot in, or towards, the number five (5) spot.
  • Also, don’t be ashamed to admit that you need training in a certain application. We all have to learn some things for the first time. If you really need training please place a dot in the number five (5) spot.

Other things:

  • If a particular application does not apply to the work you do please place a dot in the N/A column.
  • Also, after you submit the survey you will get a chance to add any comments you would like to your submitted survey. Please feel free to say anything you like.

What happens to this survey?
Once you submit the survey every dot you filled in gets recorded and added, along with everyone else’s survey, to a large database wherefrom we will "run the numbers" to determine what fields we should offer training in. Your comments will be taken into consideration as well, so don’t forget them.
